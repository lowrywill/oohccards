Card Game Test

This is a simple website that allows you to test a card game.

Sliders

There are three sliders in this section. The sliders allow you to manipulate the values. The current sum of the values is displayed at the bottom of the section.

Decks

There are two simulated decks of cards in this section. The decks will have 9 cards in predetermined order. The 'front' of each card will have text content that you provide. The 'back' of one deck will be blue, and the 'back' of the other deck will be red.

Questions

There is a text-input area where the player will answer text prompts on the cards. These should be saved so that at the end of the game they can all be exported.
